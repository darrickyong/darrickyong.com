About

I am a software engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Previously thriving in the world of investment compliance and finance, I made the life-altering decision to change my career path to tech after the birth of my first child. When it comes to solving unique and complex problems, my prior background informs my detail-oriented and risk-conscious approach, while my newfound fascination with tech allows me to have a completely new perspective.

When I'm not in front of a computer learning about data structures, tackling my next pet project or gaming, I am educating myself about child development or spending time with my wife and son. Any additional free time is spent working on my car or playing with my two cats.
