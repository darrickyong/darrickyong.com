About

I am a software engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Previously thriving in the world of investment compliance and finance, I made the life-altering decision to change my career path to software development after the birth of my first child. I have a passion for JavaScript, Rails, and React/Redux, as can be seen throughout my portfolio. I approach problems with genuine curiousity and optimism and am motivated by recognizing and crafting creative solutions for process improvements. When it comes to solving unique and complex problems, my experience from my prior career allows me to be detail-oriented and risk-conscious, while my newfound fascination with tech allows me a completely new perspective.

When I'm not in front of a computer learning about data structures, tackling my next pet project or gaming, I am educating myself about child development or spending time with my wife and son. Any additional free time is spent working on my car or playing with my two cats.
